The Village of Westmuir plays host to a number of Skills and Services. All of the services detailed below are offered locally and in most cases will be more convenient and possibly less expensive that having to look further afield. Central to the village is The Wee Shoppie where Eileen and Charles (Chunk) provide us with a huge range of daily necessities and a number of treats too, especially Chunk's delicious baking. Perhaps though you are looking for a Gardener or a Joiner or other Tradesman. If we don't already have one, then we can certainly tell you about someone in the area to you, borne out of our own experience and recommendation. Click on the links to find out more!
