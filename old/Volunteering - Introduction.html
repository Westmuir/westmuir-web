You've reached this page because you want to find out more about how you might get involved in the Westmuir Community by becoming a Volunteer. Westmuir offers a number of Volunteer Opportunities for village residents and friends:

There are 3 main areas in which you can become a Volunteer . . . .

The Village Hall and Car Park

  • Hall Maintenance
  • Car Park Maintenance
  • Fundraising Events
  • Administration Roles
  • Financial Roles
  • Hall Committee Membership
  • Be a 'Friend of the Hall'

The Community Development Trust and Woodland

  • Woodland Management
  • Woodland Car Park
  • Paths and Boardwalks
  • Strimming & Grass Cutting
  • Litter Picking
  • Financial Roles
  • Become a Trust Member
  • Join the Trust Board of Directors

Taking Care of the Village Defibrillator

  • Help Raise Awareness
  • Help with Weekly Checks
  • Kiosk Cleaning
  • Administration Role
  • Help with defibrillator / first aid awareness sessions

volunteers@westmuir.org.uk

Just email us at the above address and we'll get right back to you.

You'll be made most welcome and you won't regret it ! Our existing volunteers are a friendly bunch of folk who enjoy getting hands-on and contributing to the life of the village in a way that offers fun, friendship and a huge sense of satisfaction.
