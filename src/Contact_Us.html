How to get to Westmuir

Map and Directions

Westmuir lies on the A926 about 1 mile west of Kirriemuir in Angus, Scotland. The map opposite can be moved and zoomed in or out as required and also viewed in a variety of formats.

For Satnav users, navigate to postcode DD8 5LP.
