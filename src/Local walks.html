Westmuir sits at the foot of the Angus Glens and is an ideal starting point for many local and nearby walks. The links below will take you to a variety of routes suitable to most abilities and provide not only the starting point and route distance but also a profile of the terrain which is ideal in deciding where best to take a rest ! Our thanks to George Milne, local Westmuir resident, for sharing this rambler's resource for all to enjoy.
