The Community Development Trust is a charity which is managed by a small group of Directors all of whom live in Westmuir. The Trust is principally involved with the management of the Westmuir Community Woodland. Trust Directors meet about 4 times a year or on demand for any current development projects.

Many of the local residents are Members of the Trust and volunteer to be involved in the work of the Trust.

Woodland & Car Park Management

  • Help on Woodland Maintenance days with guidance and supervision.
  • Help keep ditches and gratings clear and free draining.
  • Help keep the surface of the woodland car park clean and free from dirt and leaves.
  • Clean and mainitain bird boxes.
  • Help a qualified team fell dangerous trees to form habitat piles to aid biodiversity.

Paths and Boardwalks

  • Keep boardwalks clear of obstructions and moss.
  • cut back vegetation encroaching on pathways and boardwalks.

Strimming and Grass Cutting

  • Be part of a team who keep the play area grass and pathway verges cut during the growing season. (Lawnmower provided)

Be a Woodland Monitor

  • Mointor woodland for any work needing done.
  • Pick litter on an ongoing basis
  • Invite dog walkers when encountered to pick up their dog's deposits and palce in bins provided.

Become a Board Director

  • Volunteer to become a Board Member and join the team which plans and coordinates all activities in connection with the woodland.
  • Consider being willing to take on a position of Chair, Secretary, Finance Director etc. if you have suitable experience in any of these roles.

Become a Trust Member

  • Support the Trust by becoming a member and offering your time and talents as a volunteer.

Our existing volunteers are a friendly bunch of folk who enjoy getting hands-on and contributing to the life of the village in a way that offers fun, friendship and a huge sense of satisfaction.

If you'd like to talk to someone about becoming a Volunteer, just email us at volunteers@westmuir.org.uk and we'll get right back to you for a chat. You'll be made most welcome and you won't regret it !
