The Village Hall and Car Park

The Village Hall and the adjacent Car Park are managed by a Hall Committee with the help of a number of 'Friends of the Hall' who volunteer on a regular basis to help run fundraising events, keep the hall and its activities in good order and maintain the village car park for on behalf of all hall users.

Maintenance of Hall and Car Park

  • Bigger jobs are done by a professional and are funded by events and hall rentals.
  • Each year a team of volunteers gives the Car Park a half day tidy up, pruning,etc.
  • Fence painting is done every few years, around the hall and garden to the rear.
  • The garden grass is cut regularly throughout the summer.

Fundraising & Publicity

  • Help plan fundraising events (asking for helpers, baking, shopping, running tombolas, etc., etc.)
  • Send event info. and posters to Web Manager, local press, posting on Facebook.
  • Setting up before an event and clearing up afterwards or next day.
  • Arrange collection of items for Christmas Raffle, making up Prize Hampers and delivering same if needed.

Administration Roles

  • Taking Hall Bookings and keeping a bookings diary at home and in the Wee Shoppie.
  • Introducing new users to the hall and briefing them on Health & Safety and Fire Regulations
  • Opening the hall for non-regular users and as a Polling Station
  • Arrange yearly checks of electrical items with electrician.

Financial Roles

  • Keep the hall bank account and annual accounts in good order.
  • Collect Hall Rents.
  • Organise Annual Insurance.
  • Assisting with Grant applications for larger hall projects.

Be a Committee Member

  • Attend committee meetings to plan upcoming events
  • Secretary role takes minutes
  • Maintain contact details forFriends of the Hall
  • Committee Members are the driving force behind most hall events and fundraisers.

Be a Friend of the Hall

  • Be willing to be contacted when extra help is needed
  • Help prepare the hall before events and tidy away afterwards
  • Donate and contribute in any way you can to events, raffles, tombolas, auctions etc.
